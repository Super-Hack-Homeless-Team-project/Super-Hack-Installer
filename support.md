## 📘 English

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Do I need internet to use the installer?**  
A: Yes. An active internet connection is required to verify your license key or account.

**Q: Where do I get my license key?**  
A: Your license key is provided after purchase. Please check your email or the platform you used to buy the mod.

**Q: Can I share my license key with friends?**  
A: No. Each license key is unique and tied to your purchase. Sharing keys may result in account suspension.

**Q: What happens if my authentication fails?**  
A: The installer will show an error message. Please check your key or account credentials and try again.

**Q: What if the installer fails to download the mod menu?**  
A: Ensure your internet connection is stable. The installer has a retry mechanism, but if the issue persists, contact support.
