# Super Hack installer
Super Hack Installer is a companion mod designed to handle authentication and installation of paid content for the **Super Hack Mod Menu**.  
This installer does not contain the full paid features itself, but instead provides a secure way for users to validate their license key or account and then download the premium mod menu directly into Geometry Dash.

---

## ✨ Features
- License key or account authentication directly inside Geometry Dash.
- Secure communication with our servers to verify ownership.
- Automatic download and installation of the **Super Hack Mod Menu** after successful validation.
- Clear feedback messages for success or failure during authentication.
- Lightweight design: only acts as a gateway, keeping the main paid mod protected.

---

## 📖 How to Use
1. Install **Super Hack Installer** through Geode.
2. Launch Geometry Dash and open the installer interface.
3. Enter your license key or log in with your account credentials.
4. If authentication is successful, the installer will automatically fetch and install the **Super Hack Mod Menu** into your game.
5. Restart Geometry Dash to enjoy the premium features.

---

## 🔒 Important Notes
- This installer requires an active internet connection to verify your license.
- Your license key or account information is never stored locally; it is only used for verification.
- Please do not share your license key with others. Each key is unique and tied to your purchase.
- Geode does not handle payments or DRM — all payment infrastructure is managed by us.
